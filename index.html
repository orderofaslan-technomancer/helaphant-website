THE HELAPHANT

What the hell
is a Helaphant?

A fusion of two ancient forces.

Hel — the fierce Norse goddess who rules the underworld with a half-decayed face and an iron will.

The Elephant — matriarch, protector, keeper of memory. The one who forms the circle when one of her own is vulnerable.

The Helaphant is the woman who holds both the savage and the sacred. She protects fiercely, loves deeply, and refuses to apologize for her power.

— This is for the women who were told they were too much.

“The purpose of life, as far as I can tell... is to find a mode of being that's so meaningful that the fact that life is suffering is no longer relevant.”

— JORDAN PETERSON
